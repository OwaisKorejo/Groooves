Android Early Access

Groooves is going through Google Play's closed testing period. Follow the steps below to get early access.

1
Join our Google Group
Sign in to your Google account, then join the group at groups.google.com/g/groooves-testers. If you see "You don't have permission", make sure you're signed in; click Sign in in the top right, then click Join Group.

Make sure you're signed in with the same Google account you use on the Play Store.
2
Install from the Play Store
On your Android device, open this link: play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.owaiskorejo.Grooves

Make sure you're signed into Play Store with the same account you used in step 1. You should now be able to install the app.

Join our Discord to share feedback. We read everything and ship updates every 2-4 days. It is not required, but it is highly appreciated.

Join Discord

Early access requires the app to be installed for a minimum of 14 days.
