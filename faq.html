Frequently Asked Questions

What is Groooves?

Groooves is a music rating and social platform. Share album reviews, build your collection, and connect with other music lovers.

How do I create an account?

Download the Groooves app from the App Store and sign up with your email address. You can add a username and profile picture during setup.

Can I edit or delete my reviews?

Yes. You have full control over your content. You can edit or delete your reviews, collections, and following list at any time from within the app.

How do I delete my account?

In the app: Go to your profile → Settings → Delete Account, then confirm.

By email: Send a deletion request to digitalclimbcorp@gmail.com with your username or account email. We process requests within 30 days.

Is Groooves available on Android?

Groooves is currently available on iOS only. We're always exploring ways to bring the app to more platforms.

How can I report inappropriate content?

You have the right to report other users for misconduct, including explicit profile pictures or offensive usernames. Use the report option in the app to notify us.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You have the right to export your data. Please email us at digitalclimbcorp@gmail.com to request a data export.

How do I contact support?

If you have any questions or need help, please reach out to us at digitalclimbcorp@gmail.com.
