User Guide

How to edit and delete your content in Groooves.

Reviews (Album & Song Reviews)

To delete a review:

  1. Tap on the review
  2. Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top right corner
  3. Tap Delete

Song reviews work the same way — they appear as part of your album review. Tap the review, use the three dots in the top right, then delete or edit.

You can also edit any review using the same menu (three dots → Edit).

Collections

To delete or edit a collection:

  1. Tap on the collection
  2. Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top right corner
  3. Tap Delete or Edit

Both delete and edit work the same way as reviews.

Edit Profile

You can edit your profile at any time. From your profile, tap the edit option to change:

  • Username
  • Profile picture
  • Banner
  • Description

Delete Comments

To delete a comment:

  1. Find the three dots (⋮) on the comment (next to the comment, not in the top right of the screen)
  2. Tap the three dots
  3. Tap Delete

Delete Account

To permanently delete your Groooves account and all associated data, see our Delete Account page for full steps and details.
